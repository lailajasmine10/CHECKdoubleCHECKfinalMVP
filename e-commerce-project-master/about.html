welcome to Emerald Vinyl!

Emerald Vinyl was created from the passion of our hearts, an ember of a fierce flame that grew to the size of a forest fire. it lives within the music notes that are gently carried by the wind. it touches our souls in a way our mind cannot comprehend. it all started from humans desire to listen to art and like a seed, it grew to an everlasting desire to spread that art to many others so they too can touch their souls with what the material world cannot.
